Jam Filled Butter Cookies

Description

These jam filled butter cookies will be a hit at your next holiday party! They are very easy to make but taste much more complex. Everyone will be asking you for the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 3/4 all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup fruit preserves, any flavor

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. In a medium bowl, cream together the butter, white sugar, and egg yolks. Mix in flour a little bit at a time until a soft dough forms.
  3. Roll dough into 1 inch balls. If dough is too soft, refigerate for 15 to 20 minutes.
  4. Place balls 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  5. Use your finger or an inustrument of similar size to make well in the center of each cookie. Fill the hole with 1/2 teaspoon of preserves.
  6. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven, until golden brown on the bottom. Remove from cookie sheets to cool on wire racks.
